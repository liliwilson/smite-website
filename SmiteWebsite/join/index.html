How to Join sMITe!

Joining sMITe is Easy!

sMITe is an all-inclusive ultimate frisbee team and we are always looking for new members! Take a look at our practice and event schedule, especially during the fall most of our practices are open to new players and designed to teach the basics!

The Social Side!

sMITe creates a bond on and off the team. With a dedicated "social captain", hosting fun events of all types is an important part of smite!

  • Fris-Formal
  • Party Chill
  • Dumpling Making
  • White Elephant / Secret Santa
  • Team dinners after practices
  • Throwing buddies
  • Casual meetups and study sessions
