Schedule

New players, feel free to come to any practice or event labeled as "open" on the schedule!

Typically, we have three practices each week, one on the weekend and two during the week in the evenings.

FAQ

Where is practice?

Practice is usually held on one of the Brigg's fields, (labeled E,D,C,B) OR on one of the turf fields, Roberts or Barry.

The schedule will indicate the field for each practice!

What should I bring?

  • Cleats/Tennis shoes
  • Water
  • A light (white) and dark shirt for scrimmages
  • Ultimate Frisbee gloves (if you like them)
  • Layers when it is cold!
