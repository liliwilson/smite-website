Welcome to MIT Women's Ultimate Frisbee

Welcome to the official homepage of sMITe, MIT Women's Ultimate Frisbee.

We are a club sport at MIT consisting of both undergraduate and graduate students. We travel throughout New England and occasionally out of region to compete against other colleges and compete in the USA Ultimate College Series. If you like being outside, working hard to play hard, and being part of a fun-loving and enthusiastic team, fill out our interest form or email us at womens-ultimate@mit.edu! We look forward to meeting you.
