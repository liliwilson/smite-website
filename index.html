Welcome to sMITe!

Welcome to the official homepage of sMITe, MIT's Ultimate Frisbee team who plays in the USAU Women's division.

We are a club sport at MIT consisting of both undergraduate and graduate students. We travel throughout New England and occasionally out of region to compete against other colleges and compete in the USA Ultimate College Series in the Women's division. If you like being outside, working hard to play hard, and being part of a fun-loving and enthusiastic team, fill out our interest form or email us at womens-ultimate@mit.edu! We look forward to meeting you.
