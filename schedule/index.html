Schedule

We typically have three practices each week, one on the weekend (Sunday) and two during the week in the evenings (Tuesday and Thursday).

FAQ

Where is practice?

Practice is usually held on one of the Briggs grass fields (labeled B, C, D, E) OR on one of the turf fields (Roberts or Barry).

The calendar will indicate the field for each practice!

What should I bring?

  • Cleats/sneakers
  • Water
  • A light (white) and dark shirt for scrimmages
  • Ultimate frisbee gloves (if you like them)
  • Layers when it is cold!
