The Frisbee Side

sMITe is an all-inclusive ultimate frisbee team and we are always looking for new players. Take a look at our practice and event schedule - during the fall, most of our practices are open to rookies and designed to teach the basics!

Gender inclusion is an important value of sMITe. With USAU's Gender Inclusion Policy, we affirm that people of all gender identities should have the freedom to participate in USA Ultimate sanctioned or championship series events in the division in which they feel most comfortable and safe based on their gender identity and should be recognized, respected and included at every level of the sport.
