Welcome to the sMITe film room!

Here marks the beginning of the sMITe film room, a place to share ultimate strategy and techniques through film study.

Ultimate strategy is constantly evolving with new “metas” and sets, and analyzing high-level play is incredibly important for developing frisbee IQ.

Ultimate techniques are often learned through experience rather than explicit teaching, which can hinder individual skill development and growth of the level of sport at large. Let’s change that, and nerd out about efficient flick mechanics…
